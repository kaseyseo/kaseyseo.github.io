Profile: Eixample

Eixample is west of Barcelona’s Gothic District on the northeast coast of Spain. Here, locals and visitors congregate on metropolitan sidewalks and roadways like Passeig de Gràcia, the heart of the district. This district of Barcelona is known for its high-end shopping, modern architectural achievements, and superior culinary scene. Head to Rambla de Catalunya for luxury retail, or wait until after dark to enjoy some of Eixample’s popular nightlife entertainment.
